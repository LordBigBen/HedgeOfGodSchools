About Us

Our Values

At Hedge Of God College, our values are centered on nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel academically, morally, and spiritually. We prioritize faith, integrity, discipline, respect, and creativity, fostering an environment where students can grow into confident leaders and positive role models in society

Our Team at Hedge Of God College is made up of highly experienced educators who are passionate about shaping the lives of young learners. Each member is devoted to offering quality education, fostering strong moral values, and helping students discover and develop their unique talents

Brief School History

The school began as an after-school lesson program called `Unique Mathematics Clinic' from 2010 to 2012. It transitioned into a full- fledged school on September 24, 2012, under the name 'God's Hedge College`.

The name of the school was later changed to "Hedge of God College' after its registration.


At its inception, the school had three teachers:

1. Mrs Ezekwueme-Bartholomew Uchenna Glory (Proprietress)
2. Mr. Kolesho Abayomi (Acting Principal)
3. Miss Ogbonna-Eze Divine Favour

The school also welcomed three students in JSS 1 at the time of resumption:
1. Babatunde Azeez
2. Babatunde Habeeb
3. Mojeed Olamide

The school was established with the vision of raising smart, knowledgeable, confident, godly. and talented boys and girls who would serve as role models in society and the world at large. Today, Hedge of God College has grown to accommodate 300+ students, supported by 20+ teaching staff and non-teaching staff members.
